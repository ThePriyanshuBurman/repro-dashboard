Service Groups

Service Groups allow you to offer specific services to specific clients.

This is a great way to offer different services/packages to specific clients, whether that's based on location if you service multiple areas, or if you simply have varying rates for different clients.

You can then assign client accounts to a specific service group to define what services they can book.

  1. Create Service Groups
    • Each Service Group is a category or bucket of services that you want to place clients into.
    • When you create your first Service Group we'll place all of your existing services into the new group.
    • We'll also place all of your existing clients into the Service Group.
    • This will allow all existing clients to book any existing service, just like it is today.
    • Your first Service Group is considered your base or default Service Group.
    • All new accounts will default to being placed in this group.
  2. Update Services
    • Under Services, modify the Service Group(s) of each service by clicking the label above the service.
    • You can place a service under one or multiple Service Groups.
  3. Update Client Accounts
    • After creating service groups, the Settings -> Accounts page will list the available service groups for each account.
    • Click on the service group button for each account to adjust that account's service groups.
    • A client can have access to none, one, or multiple Service Groups.
    • With service groups ON, a client account that is not attached to any service groups will not have access to the Book page.
  4. Enable Service Groups
    • Enable Service Groups by clicking the toggle at the top of the page.
    • When Service Group functionality is disabled, all clients that have access to the Book page can see and select from any available service during booking.
