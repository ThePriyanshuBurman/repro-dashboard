At the beginning of September 2023 we started to receive feedback that emails weren't being delivered to client inboxes.
After verifying our system was successfully delivering to email providers and continuing to hear of additional clients not receiving emails, we started to look into the issue further.
We confirmed that for email accounts who are serviced by Outlook, emails were not being placed in the inboxes.
Although Outlook receives the messages, the internal spam filter prevents the emails from being delivered to their final inbox destination.
As this only affects Outlook email clients, we have implemented a solution to get email notifications into inboxes by sending an alternate version of our emails when a Microsoft-hosted email address is attached to a shoot.
This page lists clients that have an email account hosted by Microsoft who may be affected by this issue.
The top of the page lists the status of Outlook emails.
  • Alternate Outlook Emails OFF
    Emails to Microsoft hosted accounts are being received in inboxes as expected. Standard/default email notifications are being sent to all clients.
  • Alternate Outlook Emails ON
    Emails to Microsoft hosted accounts are not being received in inboxes as expected. Alternate Outlook-safe email notifications are being used when sending to Microsoft hosted email addresses, to ensure emails reach the inbox.

We check if a client's email address is hosted by a Microsoft company.

If the email is routed through Microsoft, we flag the account as an Outlook serviced email.

If you have a client that you know uses an Outlook email account, or you aren't sure but think they aren't receiving notifications, send us a support request.

We can look into the emails that were sent to the client and determine if they may be an Outlook user or not, and if it appears the mail made it to their inbox.

For clients listed, we recommend they turn on Text Notifications by logging in to their account and clicking on My Account, and enabling the feature.

You can reach out to the clients in this list to let them know about the issue, and ask them to add noreply@viewshoot.com to their Safe Senders list.

In some cases, it may be necessary for the brokerage providing the email account to have their tech support staff enable the above Safe Sender option at the Administration level.

After monitoring emails being sent to Microsoft hosted inboxes, we have confirmed emails are arriving successfully.

We will continue to monitor this issue closely, and if necessary, revert back to our Outlook Safe Alternative Emails.

Microsoft has finally provided the solution that we have been trying to receive since September.

Their internal block list was not updating/learning successfully to allow our emails to go through, so they were finally able to intervene and update their end to prevent our emails from being blocked.

Emails have been working/sending successfully since 3/7/2024.

Since emails are going successfully to Outlook inboxes with our alternate way of sending to them, we are holding off returning emails back to normal until at least 2 weeks of successful sending.

Text Notification Feature
We have enabled a new feature for Text Notifications that are tied to only the Photo Shoot Ready & Shoot Summary emails. A text notification is sent to the client with a link to the shoot so they can access the shoot media when a shoot is finalized. This feature is available across all subscription plans.

For Your Clients
Text Notifications can be enabled by logging in to a client account and going to the My Account page. Clients need a valid phone number and will have to request a Verification Code from the button provided to enable Text Notifications. A notification will be sent when a shoot is ready (and shoot reminders will continue to be sent via text if your plan includes that functionality and you have text reminders on).

For You (Admins)
Company admins can view the Activity Log of a shoot to confirm a text message was sent to a client. You can find the Activity Log when viewing a shoot by clicking on the pulse icon to the left of the Quick Actions button, and scrolling down when the Activity Log pops up.
